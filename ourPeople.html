Klint Mane Ph.D.
(Co-founder and Advisor)

Klint earned his Ph.D. in applied economics from the University of Rochester. His research explores the influence of technology on labor markets, providing valuable insights for informed policy-making. He has used his economics expertise in several internships in the world of tech and economic consulting. Klint was a team lead for 1.5 years at Health and Environmental Economics Lab (HEEL) producing several successful reports for the US government and contributing in publishing research papers.

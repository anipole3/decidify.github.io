Healthcare

Data-driven Healthcare Analysis for Improved Patient Outcomes

Our co-founder was involved in a groundbreaking US government initiative (RECOVER - nearly 1 billion $), aimed at addressing Long-COVID—a condition affecting 7-23 millions of individuals in the US. Leveraging the largest electronic health records for COVID-19 in the US, we employed advanced econometrics and machine learning techniques to uncover crucial insights. From identifying risk factors to exploring potential treatments, our data-driven approach delivers actionable solutions for improved patient outcomes. This research was presented to the United States Congress and played a pivotal role in shaping policy decisions aimed at preventing and treating Long-COVID. Partner with us to unlock the transformative power of healthcare data analysis. (Link to the full research paper)

Logistics and Warehouse Management

Optimizing Workers' Allocation Through Simulation

Our co-founder played a pivotal role in developing a simulation framework for optimising the allocation of workers in distribution centres (DCs). Traditionally managers have faced an insurmountable challenge determining the best allocation policy given the complex nature of work and the associated constraints. However, our model faithfully replicates the actual operations of a DC and it precisely identifies the optimal allocation strategy for managers. This has resulted in a 4% increase in DC’s throughput making DC operations more cost effective. We invite you to collaborate with us and seize the opportunity to leverage this innovative solution, allowing you to optimise potential business decisions in advance.

Technology and Advertising

Transforming Market Analysis with Cutting-Edge Classification and Forecasting Models

Our co-founder's data science application modernized market analysis for a leading electronics company. By building a classification model using machine learning, we accurately categorized millions of Google searches, saving over 30 hours per week in manual categorization efforts. With an impressive 95% accuracy rate, our model enabled us to gain a comprehensive view of market trends. Leveraging historical data, we also developed a forecasting model that provided accurate predictions of future demand for each electronics category. The client utilized the analysis to strategically allocate millions of dollars in advertisement funds across various geographical locations and products. Experience the power of data-driven decision-making today with cutting edge machine learning models.
